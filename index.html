About Us

We Build Websites That Actually Work.

Based in Kathmandu, Nepal — we're a small team that builds clean, fast websites for businesses. No bloated processes, no corporate overhead. Just good design and solid code.

What We Believe

Your website shouldn't look like everyone else's. We believe in building things that are honest, functional, and actually reflect who you are as a business. No cookie-cutter templates. No bloated page builders. Just clean, intentional work.

How We Work

We keep it simple. You tell us what you need, we figure out the best way to build it, and we keep you in the loop the whole way. No jargon. No surprises. Just straightforward communication and honest timelines.

Why We Started This

We noticed too many small businesses getting ripped off by expensive agencies or stuck with terrible DIY websites. So we started Pix N Patch — to give every business access to quality web design at a fair price. Simple as that.

How We Work

Our Development Process

No complicated workflows or red tape. Here's how we take your project from idea to launch.

01

Discover

We sit down (or hop on a call) and learn about your business, your goals, and what you actually need.

02

Plan

We map out the structure, pages, and features so there are no surprises down the road.

03

Design

We create a design that matches your brand and looks better than anything off-the-shelf.

04

Develop

We write clean code and build your site. You get updates along the way so nothing feels like a black box.

05

Test

We test everything — on phones, tablets, laptops, different browsers. If something breaks, we fix it.

06

Launch

We put your site live, make sure everything works, and hand it over with a smile.

07

Support

Your site's alive and kicking — but we're still here if you need changes, updates, or just a helping hand.
